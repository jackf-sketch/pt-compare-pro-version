How PTCompare Pro Works

We show every patient exactly what they will pay before stepping foot in a clinic — insurance path or cash path, session by session.

3 Steps to Affordable PT

1

Search Your Location

Enter any US zip code or city. PTCompare Pro queries Google Places in real time and returns every licensed PT clinic nearby with live ratings, hours, and addresses.

2

Configure the Cost Calculator

Select your insurance provider, enter your remaining deductible and out-of-pocket maximum, then drag the sessions slider to match your expected plan of care. The engine recalculates every card instantly.

3

Compare Bars and Book

Each clinic card shows two color-coded progress bars — your estimated insurance out-of-pocket vs. the flat cash rate — so you can see at a glance which path saves money. Click Book Appointment to request a session directly.

How the Cost Calculator Works

Our financial model processes each session individually, mirroring how a real insurance claim moves through your plan year:

📋

Deductible Spend-Down

Early sessions cost you the full negotiated rate until your deductible is met. We apply each session's cost against the remaining deductible one by one.

💵

Coinsurance After Deductible

Once the deductible is met you pay only your coinsurance percentage — typically 15 to 25 percent — on each additional session.

🛡️

Out-of-Pocket Maximum

Once your OOP max is hit the insurer covers 100 percent. The calculator stops adding patient costs at the exact session where you cross this cap.

📊

Network Contract Multipliers

Each insurer negotiates different contract rates with providers. We apply plan-specific multipliers derived from published CMS and insurer data for all 20 supported plans.

Accuracy note: Estimates are based on average industry contract rates and plan structures. Your actual costs depend on your specific plan, network status, visit caps, and local market. Always verify with your insurer and clinic before treatment. PTCompare Pro costs are estimates for comparison only — not guaranteed quotes.

Premium Modality Coverage Override

Some modalities — including Shockwave Therapy, Myofascial Cupping, Blood Flow Restriction training, and ASTYM — are typically excluded from standard insurance coverage. When you check one of these in the filter panel, our system automatically triggers a coverage override alert and recalculates both bars using full out-of-pocket cash rates. This prevents the misleading scenario where a patient assumes insurance will cover a service it typically does not.

Coverage Override Active: the selected modality is typically not covered by standard insurance plans. Insurance bar now reflects full out-of-pocket cost. Cash package rate shown for comparison.

Why Cash Pay Is Often Cheaper

With Insurance Before Deductible
You pay the full negotiated rate each session — typically $95 to $180. Billing takes 30 to 90 days and surprise EOBs are common.
With Cash Pay
Many clinics offer flat cash packages at $65 to $140 per session with no surprise bills and no prior authorization requirements.
